# userscripts
 custom userscripts for tamper/grease/violent monkeys

# installation
1. install a userscript manager extension for your browser, such as [Tampermonkey](https://www.tampermonkey.net/) or [Violentmonkey](https://violentmonkey.github.io/).
2. click on the userscript file you want to install:

| File Name | Description
| --- | ---
| [twitter_x_start_on_news_tab.user.js](https://github.com/Araxeus/userscripts/raw/main/browser_snippets/twitter_x_start_on_news_tab.user.js) | Automatically clicks the 'news' tab on Twitter (X) to show news content by default.
| [twitter_video_download_button.user.js](https://github.com/Araxeus/userscripts/raw/main/browser_snippets/twitter_video_download_button.user.js) | Adds a download button to Twitter (X) videos for easy downloading.
| [twitch_clips_download_button.user.js](https://github.com/Araxeus/userscripts/raw/main/browser_snippets/twitch_clips_download_button.user.js) | Adds a download button to Twitch clips for easy downloading.
| [reddit_download_video_button.user.js](https://github.com/Araxeus/userscripts/raw/main/browser_snippets/reddit_download_video_button.user.js) | Adds a download button to Reddit posts with videos.
| [imgur_alternative_frontend_redirect.user.js](https://github.com/Araxeus/userscripts/raw/main/browser_snippets/imgur_alternative_frontend_redirect.user.js) | Redirects Imgur to an alternative frontend. (for those that imgur is a blocked domain, or just want to avoid the ads and trackers)
| [imgur_alternative_frontend_redirect_inside_script.user.js](https://github.com/Araxeus/userscripts/raw/main/browser_snippets/imgur_alternative_frontend_redirect_inside_script.user.js) | open the image itself in the alternative frontend
| [redgifs_downloadButton.user.js](https://github.com/Araxeus/userscripts/raw/main/browser_snippets/redgifs_downloadButton.user.js) | Adds a download button to Redgifs videos for easy downloading.
| [hltv_viewer_count.user.js](https://github.com/Araxeus/userscripts/raw/main/browser_snippets/hltv_viewer_count.user.js) | Displays the viewer count on HLTV match pages.
| [hackernews_expand_all.user.js](https://github.com/Araxeus/userscripts/raw/main/browser_snippets/hackernews_expand_all.user.js) | Expands all comments on Hacker News threads.
| [galatz_volume_scroll_bynetcdn.com.user.js](https://github.com/Araxeus/userscripts/raw/main/browser_snippets/galatz_volume_scroll_bynetcdn.com.user.js) | Enables volume scrolling on Galatz radio player
| [disable_ads_mobalytics.gg.user.js](https://github.com/Araxeus/userscripts/raw/main/browser_snippets/disable_ads_mobalytics.gg.user.js) | Disables ads on Mobalytics.gg
| [cloudflare-domain-finder.user.js](https://github.com/Araxeus/userscripts/raw/main/browser_snippets/cloudflare-domain-finder.user.js) | Finds domains using Cloudflare
| [13tv.co.il_volume_scroll.user.js](https://github.com/Araxeus/userscripts/raw/main/browser_snippets/13tv.co.il_volume_scroll.user.js) | Enables volume scrolling on 13tv.co.il
| [mako_volume_scroll.user.js](https://github.com/Araxeus/userscripts/raw/main/browser_snippets/mako_volume_scroll.user.js) | Enables volume scrolling on Mako.co.il
| [kan.org.il_volume_scroll.user.js](https://github.com/Araxeus/userscripts/raw/main/browser_snippets/kan.org.il_volume_scroll.user.js) | Enables volume scrolling on Kan.org.il
